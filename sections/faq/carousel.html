Common Questions

Before you call

How fast can you get here?

Most calls are scheduled same day. We keep slots open for urgent repairs, so call early and we'll do our best to be there today.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — every estimate is free and comes in writing, with no obligation and no pressure.

Are you licensed and insured?

Fully licensed and insured in Florida. We're happy to provide documentation before any work begins.

What if the repair costs more than quoted?

We call you first — always. Nothing beyond the quoted scope gets done without your approval.
